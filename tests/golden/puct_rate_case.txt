DOCKET NO. 56789
APPLICATION OF ONCOR ELECTRIC DELIVERY COMPANY LLC FOR AUTHORITY TO CHANGE RATES

TO THE HONORABLE COMMISSIONERS OF THE PUBLIC UTILITY COMMISSION OF TEXAS:

Oncor Electric Delivery Company LLC ("Oncor" or "Company") respectfully files
this Application for Authority to Change Rates pursuant to PURA §§ 36.001-36.159
and PUC Substantive Rules 25.231 and 25.241. Oncor requests that the Commission
authorize changes to its base rates as described herein.

I. INTRODUCTION

Oncor requests the Commission authorize new rates that will produce revenues
approximately $1.1 billion higher than current revenues on an annualized basis.
The requested increase is driven by substantial capital investments in
transmission and distribution infrastructure made since the Company's last rate
case and necessary to maintain system reliability and accommodate the significant
load growth occurring throughout Oncor's service territory, particularly from data
centers and large industrial facilities in North Texas.

II. BACKGROUND

Oncor serves approximately 10 million customers across 400 communities and more
than 140,000 square miles in Texas, including 13 of the 20 fastest-growing
counties in the United States. Since Oncor's last rate case (Docket No. 49494,
final order issued January 2020), the Company has invested approximately $7.5
billion in capital improvements, including high-voltage transmission line
upgrades, substation construction and expansion, distribution automation
equipment, and grid hardening measures required under PUCT weatherization rules
adopted following Winter Storm Uri.

III. RELIEF REQUESTED

Oncor requests that the Commission:
1. Authorize a total annual revenue requirement of approximately $4.2 billion;
2. Set an authorized return on equity of 9.65 percent;
3. Approve updated depreciation rates as set forth in Exhibit OEC-5;
4. Authorize implementation of interim rates pending a final order, effective
   60 days from the filing date;
5. Establish final rates with an effective date of 60 days following issuance of
   a final order; and
6. Address all other matters necessary and appropriate to implement new rates.

IV. COMPLIANCE WITH PURA AND COMMISSION RULES

This Application complies with all provisions of the Public Utility Regulatory
Act (PURA) and applicable Commission rules, including PUC Substantive Rules
25.231 and 25.241. Oncor has served all required parties and filed all required
exhibits simultaneously with this Application as required by Rule 25.231(c).
