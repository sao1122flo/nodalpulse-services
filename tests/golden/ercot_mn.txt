ERCOT Market Notice MN-2026-05-0147
Category: Market Operations
Issued: May 13, 2026
Priority: NORMAL

SUBJECT: Settlement Invoice Correction — April 2026 Final Settlement

ERCOT is issuing this Market Notice to inform Qualified Scheduling Entities
(QSEs) and other affected market participants of a correction to the April 2026
Final Settlement invoices that will be reissued on May 20, 2026.

I. DESCRIPTION OF ISSUE

ERCOT identified a calculation error in the Non-Spinning Reserve (Non-Spin)
settlement for operating hours 0200-0600 Central Time on April 3, 4, and 5,
2026. The error affects 47 QSEs that provided Non-Spin or ERCOT Contingency
Reserve Service (ECRS) during those hours.

The RRSN settlement calculation incorrectly applied a 4-hour performance
evaluation period rather than the correct 2-hour performance period specified in
Section 6.5.9 of the Nodal Operating Protocols for those operating hours.
This error resulted in overpayments to QSEs with high Non-Spin performance scores
and underpayments to QSEs with lower performance scores during the affected
intervals. The net market-wide settlement error is approximately $2.3 million.

II. CORRECTIVE ACTION

ERCOT will reprocess the April 2026 Final Settlement for the affected hours and
issue corrected invoices to all 47 affected QSEs. QSEs that were overpaid will
see a debit on their corrected invoice; QSEs that were underpaid will see a
credit. Net correction amounts range from approximately -$180,000 to +$95,000
across the affected entities.

III. TIMELINE

- Corrected invoices issued to affected QSEs: May 20, 2026
- Payment adjustment via wire transfer processed by ERCOT: May 27, 2026
- Dispute deadline for corrected April Final Settlement invoices: June 10, 2026

IV. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

QSEs wishing to verify their specific correction amounts may contact the ERCOT
Market Operations Center. ERCOT will post the list of affected QSEs and their
correction amounts (in aggregate, without individual identification) to the ERCOT
MIS portal no later than May 16, 2026.
